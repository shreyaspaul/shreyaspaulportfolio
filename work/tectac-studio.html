Overview

TecTac Studio is a web development and design agency that needed a website to attract new clients and showcase their services.

The website needed to convey a conversational tone, guiding users through a sales pitch that would explain how TecTac can help solve their problems. The website's primary goal was to convert visitors into leads, and eventually into customers.
